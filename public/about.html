PLINY

Civic correspondence reimagined

About
Pliny is an online platform connecting citizens with their government.

This website is named after the Roman author Pliny the Younger, who was a prolific writer of letters - many of which were addressed to the Emperor Trajan. His works remind us of the powerful effect written correspondence can have on government and politics.

Pliny allows the modern citizen to communicate their concerns and ideas - as effectively as Pliny the Younger did - with a suite of easy-to-use tools.

RepSearch:

Powered by the Google Civic Information API, RepSearch allows users to quickly find the mailing addresses, phone numbers, emails, websites, and social media accounts of U.S. representatives and leaders. After specifying their locality, users find a detailed contact list for local, state, and national-level policy-makers.


NewsHub:

If users are unsure of what to write or want to discuss the latest events, they can find inspiration in the latest news from News API featured in the NewsHub.
